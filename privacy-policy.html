Information We Collect

Voice of the Stars - Counsel ("the App") collects minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.
  • Chat History: Your conversations with AI personas are stored locally on your device only.
  • Language Preferences: Your selected language preference is stored locally.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered advice from historical figures
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements

Data Storage

  • All chat history and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload your conversations to external servers
  • Data is processed through secure AI APIs (xAI/Grok) for generating responses only

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • xAI (Grok API): For generating AI responses

Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all chat history through the app settings
  • Change language preferences at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

수집하는 정보

별들의 목소리 ("앱")은 서비스 제공에 필요한 최소한의 정보만 수집합니다:

  • 대화 기록: AI 페르소나와의 대화는 오직 사용자 기기에만 저장됩니다.
  • 언어 설정: 선택한 언어 설정은 로컬에 저장됩니다.

  • 역사적 인물의 AI 기반 조언 제공

데이터 저장
